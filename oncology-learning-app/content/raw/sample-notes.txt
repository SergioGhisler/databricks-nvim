QUIZ-MCQ: Which stage generally indicates bone marrow involvement in lymphoma staging systems?
- Stage III
- Stage IV
- Stage V
- Stage I
ANSWER: Stage V
EXPLAIN: Stage V generally includes bone marrow, blood, or other non-lymphoid organ involvement.

QUIZ-SHORT: List two common dose-limiting toxicities to monitor during CHOP-like protocols.
KEYWORDS: neutropenia, gastrointestinal toxicity, thrombocytopenia, febrile neutropenia
EXPLAIN: Hematologic and GI toxicities are commonly monitored and can drive dose adjustments.
